About Me


Born in New York City & raised in New Jersey. Studied at Don Bosco Preparatory High School in Ramsey, NJ; Graduated from Seton Hall University in South Orange, NJ, with a Bachelor's in Biology & a minor in Physics followed by a Master's in Healthcare Administration. Currently on track for a Doctorate in Physical Therapy.

Competitive swimmer for 20 years, & currently an Olympic candidate on the National Philippine swim team. Backstroke captain, also specializing in the 50/100m sprint freestyle events. Works as a coach/lifeguard/& swim-instructor at a Fitness & Wellness Center in NJ.

Hobbies include playing the guitar/piano, recording videos, & binge-watching anime. Favorite genres are action/adventure & sports. Loves Korean food a whole lot! Has two younger sisters who also love to swim.
